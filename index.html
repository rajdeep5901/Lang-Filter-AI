How It Works

Four stages turn raw microphone input into a clean, language-filtered stream.

01
🎤

Microphone

Live audio is captured in real time via sounddevice.

02
🧠

Whisper AI

OpenAI Whisper transcribes and identifies the spoken language.

03
⚖️

Language Decision

The detected language is compared against your chosen target.

04
🔊

Audio Output

Capabilities

Features

Built for accuracy, responsiveness, and clean audio.

🌐

18+ Languages

Detects English, Hindi, Odia, Bengali, Tamil, Telugu, Spanish, French, Japanese, Chinese and more.

⚡

Real-Time Processing

🧠

Whisper AI

Uses OpenAI's state-of-the-art multilingual speech model for robust identification.

📊

Confidence Filtering

A tunable confidence threshold prevents noisy or uncertain switches.

🎚️
